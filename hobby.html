Hobbies and Passions

A talented artist, my mother started painting at an early age. Some of those striking works still hang in our home, and she's also a gifted seamstress and fabric designer. One of her most enduring hobbies is her penchant for designing and creating striking jewelry that always elicits compliments and remarks everywhere she goes.

In addition to these talents, she also enjoys cooking (her apple pie is a thing of absolute legend) and raising dogs. If there is any truth to the adage that dogs mimic their owners, they are the absolute sweetest, most hilarious animals you'll find most anywhere.

All of the passions mentioned above pale, however, when compared to her true passion for service and helping others. I have never met a more driven individual, and I've met many, many people: billionaires, celebrities, CEOs, you name it. I often joke that, if my mom had been of a more darker ambition, she'd be the Queen of the World. She just smiles and says that she wouldn't have her life be any other way.
